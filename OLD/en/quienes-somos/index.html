About Us

Home, house, village, inhabited place… what KER means.

This term comes from the old Breton language and was used from the 11th to the 14th Centuries. It has been used to name our group, since it embodies, in three letters, what we expect our guest to experience when staying at our hotels: to feel at home, comfortable and at ease, but with the added value achieved through the dedicated service of each of the brand’s collaborators, who understand that their main goal every day is to turn each visit into the perfect stay.

This word reflects the idiosyncrasy of the hotel’s founders and the working style they instill in their offerings.

The KER Group is a family business with vast experience in tourism and hospitality.

As its members became professional architects and decorators, the KER Group acquired knowledge and experience in areas that were not explored up until that time. Thus, the KER HOTELS chain was created with the same spirit of integration and growth that epitomized it from the beginning, based on a philosophy that combines experience, professionalism and dedication to service.

The Group is committed to remaining in this industry for the long term and expects to become a leader in the industry development and management in the Americas.


Our History

2017
Ker San Telmo Hotel
The chain’s fourth hotel is currently under construction in the San Telmo neighborhood, adjacent to Puerto Madero, where the heart of Buenos Aires city’s tourism is located, with easy access to the main city spots. The project includes 120 rooms, a Health Club with a swimming pool and spa, rooms for business and social events, and a parking lot.
2009
Ker Urquiza
It is strategically located in the northern area of Buenos Aires city, 5 minutes from Belgrano neighborhood, and 10 minutes from the Panamericana and General Paz highways, with direct connections to the domestic and international airports.
2006
Ker Recoleta
In 2006, the most ambitious project until then was inaugurated: a 4-star hotel located in the Recoleta neighborhood, one of the most exclusive areas in the city.
2001
Ker Belgrano

The first project was a small hotel in the Belgrano neighborhood that has grown to become a landmark in the area today. It is located in the heart of Belgrano, one of the quaintest and most active residential neighborhoods in the City, the Hotel is located just one block from Cabildo Avenue.
